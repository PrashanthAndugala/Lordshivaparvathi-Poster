Lord Shiva and Maa Parvathi

Shiva and Parvati's story is one of divine love, devotion, and reunion: After Sati (Parvati's previous incarnation) self-immolated, Lord Shiva retreated into deep meditation, lost to the world, but Parvati, her reincarnation, was determined to marry him, undertaking intense penance (tapasya) to win his heart, a devotion so pure it eventually roused him from his trance, leading to their iconic union as Shiva-Shakti, the balance of masculine and feminine energy.
